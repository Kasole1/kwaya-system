📋 1. UTANGULIZI

Kusudi na vipengele vya mfumo

🎯 Kusudi la Mfumo

Mfumo wa Kwaya Mt. Bonifasi ni application ya kisasa iliyobuniwa kusimamia shughuli zote za kwaya kwa ufanisi mkubwa.

🔧 Teknolojia

Python 3.10+, Flask 2.3.3, SQLite3, Chart.js

✨ Vipengele

Wanakwaya, Ada Calendar, Mahudhurio, Mapato, Ratiba, Media

🔐 2. KUINGIA MFUMO

Jinsi ya kuingia na kuanza kutumia mfumo

1
Fungua browser na nenda kwenye http://127.0.0.1:5000
2
Ingiza username: admin na password: admin123
3
Mara ya kwanza, badilisha password yako
⚠️ MUHIMU: Badilisha password mara ya kwanza kuingia!

📊 3. DASHBOARD

Muhtasari wa shughuli zote

Dashboard inaonyesha: Stats Cards, Charts (Mapato, Mahudhurio, Penalty), Sidebar Widgets (Calendar, Weather, Countdown, Birthday), na Quick Actions.

💡 TIP: Unaweza kubadilisha mwonekano wa saa kwa kubonyeza 🕐

👥 4. USIMAMIZI WA WANAKWAYA

Kuongeza, kuhariri, kusimamisha, kufuta wanakwaya

1
Nenda kwenye menyu Wanakwaya
2
Bonyeza ➕ Mwanakwaya Mpya
3
Jaza fomu (Jina, Simu, Sauti)
4
Bonyeza "Hifadhi"

🔢 Member Number System

Soprano: S001, Alto: A001, Tenor: T001, Bass: B001

💰 5. ADA NA MALIPO

Kalenda ya ada na mfumo wa FIFO

🟢 Kijani - Imelipwa 100%
🟡 Manjano - Imelipwa sehemu
🔵 Bluu - Ziada
🔴 Nyekundu - Hajalipa

💰 FIFO Logic

Malipo yanaenda kulipa madeni ya zamani kwanza. Mfano: Deni la Feb 5,000 na March 5,000, ukilipa 8,000 - Feb inalipwa kamili (5,000), March sehemu (3,000).

💡 TIP: Bonyeza seli ya mwezi kulipa ada
